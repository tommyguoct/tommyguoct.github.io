Brief Bio

Chongtao Guo received the B.S. degree in Electronical and Information Engineering and the Ph.D. degree in Communication and Information Systems from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2009 and 2014, respectively. Since 2014, he has been with the College of Information Engineering, Shenzhen University, Shenzhen, China, where he is currently an assistant professor. From December 2017, he has been a postdoctoral researcher in the Information Transmission and Processing Laboratory (ITP) with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA USA.
